FAQ

1. Who can attend these free sessions?

Students are prescreened by our partners.


2. Are your tutors volunteers?

Yes, all our tutors work for free, though may be compensation for minor travel expenses.


3. Are you substituting for schoolwork?

No. We aim to complement schoolwork by providing a safe space where teenagers can finish their homework, ask questions and fill in the gaps in their knowledge.


4. How do you select the tutors?

Tutors are top ranking degree qualified students who have passed police safety checks.


5. What coursework is covered in tutorials?

We aim to complement work done at school. Students will be tested for areas of weakness and these will be focussed on directly in tutorials. Tutors are also available to help with homework and study.


6. I would like to donate, where would my money go?

We aim measure all tutor hours directly. We expect to tutor a teenager for an hour for around 50 cents. This goes towards classroom hire, tutor travel expenses (bus and train fairs) and teaching materials.
